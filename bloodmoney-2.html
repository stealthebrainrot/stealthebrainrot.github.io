About BloodMoney 2

In BloodMoney 2, players take care of Harvey Harvington, now a lost computer program. You are paid to perform basic interactions like feeding and petting, but every action has lasting consequences.

The game also features five unique minigames, providing new challenges and breaking up tension. Each choice you make affects the narrative, creating a suspenseful, emotionally engaging experience.

Key Features

  • Deep narrative: Piece together Harvey’s fragmented memory to uncover the truth behind the booth from the first game.

  • Immersive voice acting: Dexter brings Harvey to life with chilling and realistic performances.

  • Two distinct endings: Your decisions shape the outcome, offering high replay value.

  • Challenging minigames: Five mini-games add variety and test your skills.

  • Psychological depth: Explore guilt, responsibility, and ethical dilemmas in every interaction.

Ultimate Goal

The ultimate goal of BloodMoney 2 is to uncover the dark truth and confront your own responsibility. Will you seek redemption, or will past actions lead to a tragic outcome?
