About Bloodmoney

BLOODMONEY! is an indie horror clicker where every click draws players deeper into moral chaos and consequences. Released on August 3, 2025, the game combines addictive clicker mechanics with dark humor, creating a tense, unpredictable, and highly engaging experience.

The Story of BLOODMONEY!

You play as a protagonist facing a life-threatening medical crisis, needing $25,000 for urgent surgery. On the way home, Harvey Harvington appears with a sign reading “1 CLICK = 1 DOLLAR.” Every click tests your ethics—take the safe path or escalate the harm to earn faster?

Gameplay – Clicking Through Moral Chaos

  • Click to earn USD: Each click brings you closer to the $25,000 goal.

  • Seven attack tools: From the subtle Feather to the extreme Gun, increasing earnings but also moral tension.

  • Strategic decisions: Balance speed of earning and ethical consequences.

  • Simple controls: Left-click to generate money, escalation slider, pause button.

Audio, Visuals, and Experience

  • Visuals: Minimalist yet detailed animations highlight the consequences of each choice.

  • Audio: Dark soundscapes mixed with playful cues create suspense and humor.

  • Interactions: Harvey reacts to each action, delivering tension and dark comedy.

Multiple Endings – Your Choices Matter

  • Good Ending: Achieve the goal without causing harm, rewarding restraint.

  • Normal Ending: Partial success with moral compromise.

  • Bad Ending: Extreme escalation leads to severe consequences and moral warning.

Why BLOODMONEY! Is Worth Playing

  • Addictive clicker mechanics with escalating moral tension.

  • Dark humor blended with horror for a unique experience.

  • Multiple choices and endings encourage replayability.

  • Simple controls, but deep strategic gameplay.

BLOODMONEY! is a dark, thrilling clicker adventure where every click counts, challenging your decision-making and daring in a chaotic, morally charged world.

After experiencing the moral storm and chaos of BLOODMONEY!, BLOODMONEY 2 takes you on an even more brutal journey, where every click determines fate and the moral challenge is multiplied.
